Hi! I am a research assistant in the Institute of Information Science research group at Academia Sinica, Taiwan, advised by Prof. Bo-Yin Yang.

My interests lie in cryptography and quantum computing, with a focus on real-world application. Recently, I have been working on the implementation and formal verification of post-quantum algorithms, specifically optimizing sntrup761 for OpenSSH.

Prior to coming to the Academia Sinica, I completed my B.S with honors in Computer Science at National Central University (NCU), Taiwan. where I was lucky to have the chances to work with Prof. Jehn-Ruey Jiang in distributed Shor’s algorithm and Prof. Szu-Cheng Cheng in quantum pairs trading.
